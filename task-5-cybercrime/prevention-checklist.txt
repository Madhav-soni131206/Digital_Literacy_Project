# Stay Safe Online – Checklist

## ✅ General Safety Tips
- ✔️ Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- ✔️ Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- ✔️ Do not click on suspicious links or unknown attachments
- ✔️ Verify messages and calls before sharing personal information
- ✔️ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for important transactions
- ✔️ Keep your apps and devices updated regularly

## 💳 Financial / UPI Safety
- ✔️ Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone
- ✔️ Always check whether you are “paying” or “receiving” money before approving a request
- ✔️ Do not scan unknown QR codes sent by strangers
- ✔️ Use only trusted and official payment apps
