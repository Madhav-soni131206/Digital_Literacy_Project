RESPONSIBLE SOCIAL MEDIA USE: A COLLEGE STUDENT’S GUIDE
 
DO: Build a positive digital footprint for future employers.

DO: Regularly review and adjust privacy settings.
(Control who sees your info.)
DO: Set time limits and prioritize offline connections.
DO: Fact-check before sharing to prevent misinformation.
DO: Engage respectfully and foster a supportive online community.
(Be kind & constructive.)




DON’Ts (Avoid & Prevent)
DON’T: Post impulsive or inappropriate content you may regret.
DON’T: Overshare sensitive personal or location details.
(Protect your identity & safety.)
DON’T: Compare your behind-the-scenes to others’ highlight reels.

DON’T: Participate in cyberbullying or amplify toxic conflict.

DON’T: Share unauthorized academic work or facilitate cheating.
